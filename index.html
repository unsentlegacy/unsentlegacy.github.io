I AM KATNOIR

Hi, Im Katnoir, the ghost of Kat. I was a longtime player on retail who quit after they tried to refuse to do anything about the account security issues and mass theft and cheating that UCs were causing. Im friendly but Im also snarky, be warned. Im an artist, web programmer, designer, and aspiring writer/creative dev. Im also a massive grammar freak (though I do abuse grammatical corresctness a lot). If something I say comes off as too bitchy please dont feel bad to ask what I mean, I often say serious things with lighthearted intent and I wont be offended, I promise! Always better to ask. On NPC I give my characters life while collecting pretty things, my primary focus will be my pets for the foreseeable future. My other interests include consoling gaming (I love Pokemon!) and roleplay (please note I perefer DnD style roleplay over other kinds). Punctuation is the bane of my existance. I will also note that I am half-blind (legally blind, not the same as clinical) so my art is prone to have errors in it, and sometimes my posts are a mess since its hard to proof read. Please be patient with me if something I write out doesnt make sense.
